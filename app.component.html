Embed key functionality from any number of apps to your favorite app of choice in a way that makes the most sense for your workflows. With aapi you can have the ability to control the functionalities you need, when you need them, without the need to switch between multiple apps.

RIGHT FUNCTION FOR THE RIGHT DATA

aapi gathers the relevant data from virtually any app and automatically embed the correct real-time actions necessary for the event at hand.

As an example, aapi can enable actions within a PagerDuty incident page when suspicious activity is reported within Okta. From the incident page, assigned staff can suspend the user within Okta, send a message via Slack, and/or create a ticket in Zendesk, all automatically with a single click from within PagerDuty.

STEP 4

With just a single click, actions tied to real-time data take place in the connected apps. Better yet, tedious tasks and repetitive data entry are automated by aapi. No more switching between multiple apps and no more copying and pasting the same data numerous times. One click from your app of choice and your task is done.
